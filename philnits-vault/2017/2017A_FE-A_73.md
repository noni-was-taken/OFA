# 2017A_FE-A_73

Which of the following is an example of Consumer Generated Media (CGM)?

a) A website of a company through which the company publishes information about its management and financial status, as well as performance trends for individual investors
b) A website of a company through which the company transmits information about the characteristics and usage methods of its products to the general consumers
c) A website of a government organization through which the agency publishes information about its policies and administrative services to the general citizens
d) A website such as a blog and SNS, etc. through which an individual transmits information to the general public about the evaluation of products, etc. that he/she has used
?
d) A website such as a blog and SNS, etc. through which an individual transmits information to the general public about the evaluation of products, etc. that he/she has used
### Explanation

**CGM (Consumer Generated Media)** refers to content created and published by **ordinary users / consumers** — such as blog posts, social media updates, product reviews, and forum discussions.

Options A, B, and C all describe content **officially published by organizations** (companies or government agencies), which is not CGM. Only option D — individuals sharing their experiences via blogs and SNS — qualifies as consumer-generated media.
