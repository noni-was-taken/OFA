# 2017S_FE-A_41
Bob and Alice encrypt all of their communications. The encryption offers secrecy, but it is impossible to be 100% sure they are communicating with each other, nor can they verify the integrity of the messages. Which of the following types of encryption methods are Bob and Alice using?

a) AES (Advanced Encryption Standard)
b) DES (Data Encryption Standard)
c) One-time pad
d) Stream cipher
?
c) One-time pad
### Explanation

The **one-time pad (OTP)** is theoretically **perfectly secret** — provably unbreakable when used correctly. However, it provides:
- **No authentication**: you cannot verify who sent the message
- **No integrity**: you cannot detect if the message was altered

This matches the description. AES and DES can be combined with MACs for integrity verification, distinguishing them from the OTP's perfect secrecy with no built-in authentication.
