# 2017A_FE-A_71

The sales price of products is decided according to the different sales price setting methods. Which of the following approaches sets a high sales price by using the consumer psychology that high quality equals to high price?

a) Cost plus pricing
b) Differential Pricing
c) Marketing price following pricing
d) Prestige pricing
?
d) Prestige pricing
### Explanation

**Prestige pricing** (also called premium pricing) deliberately sets prices **high** to signal quality and exclusivity. It leverages the consumer perception that a higher price indicates a superior product. Luxury brands use this approach extensively.

The other options use different pricing logics:
- **Cost plus** → price = cost + markup (cost-driven, not psychology-driven)
- **Differential pricing** → different prices for different customer segments
- **Market price following** → pricing based on competitors' prices
