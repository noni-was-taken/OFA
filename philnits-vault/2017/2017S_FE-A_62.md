# 2017S_FE-A_62
Which of the following is an appropriate feasibility evaluation when checking whether the lifetime benefits of the proposed information system is greater than its lifetime costs?

a) Economic feasibility
b) Operational feasibility
c) Scheduling feasibility
d) Technical feasibility
?
a) Economic feasibility
### Explanation

**Economic feasibility** evaluates whether the **financial benefits** of a proposed system outweigh its **total costs** over its lifetime — essentially a cost-benefit analysis.

- **Operational feasibility**: assesses whether the system will work in practice and be accepted by users
- **Scheduling feasibility**: assesses whether the project can be delivered on time
- **Technical feasibility**: assesses whether the technology and expertise exist
