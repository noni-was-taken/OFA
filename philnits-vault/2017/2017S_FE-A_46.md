# 2017S_FE-A_46
Companies usually provide a means for their mobile workforce to access the corporate network securely over the Internet through insecure channels such as open wireless networks in hotels or coffee shops. Which of the following is the appropriate technology that can be used for this purpose?

a) DMZ (DeMilitarized Zone)
b) SNMP (Simple Network Management Protocol)
c) VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol)
d) VPN (Virtual Private Network)
?
d) VPN (Virtual Private Network)
### Explanation

A **VPN** creates an **encrypted tunnel** over a public network, allowing remote workers to securely access the corporate network as if physically present — even over untrusted Wi-Fi.

- **DMZ**: a network segment separating public-facing servers from the internal network
- **SNMP**: a protocol for monitoring and managing network devices
- **VoIP**: technology for transmitting voice calls over the Internet
