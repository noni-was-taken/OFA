# 2017A_FE-A_61

Which of the following situation is judged as appropriate when conducting an audit on a Business Continuity Plan (BCP)?

a) A BCP having the same standard level is applied to all business operations without any prioritization.
b) An emergency contact list of employees is created and updated to the latest version.
c) During normal times, the BCP is not disclosed to employees.
d) The important documents are stored collectively at one place without making copies.
?
b) An emergency contact list of employees is created and updated to the latest version.
### Explanation

Maintaining a **current emergency contact list** is a fundamental, auditable BCP element — it ensures that the right people can be reached quickly during a crisis.

The other options represent BCP failures:
- **A** → a BCP should **prioritize** critical business functions; uniform treatment is inefficient
- **C** → employees must be **trained and aware** of the BCP for it to be effective; concealing it defeats its purpose
- **D** → important documents should be **backed up and stored in multiple locations** to survive a disaster at any single site
