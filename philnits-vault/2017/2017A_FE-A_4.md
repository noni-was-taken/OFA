# 2017A_FE-A_4

Analog audio data is being sampled and converted to 8-bit digital audio data using the PCM method, then transmitted in 64,000 bits per second. What is the sampling interval in microseconds?

a) 15.6
b) 46.8
c) 125
d) 128
?
c) 125
### Explanation

**Step 1: Find samples per second**

Each sample = 8 bits. At 64,000 bits/second:

$$\text{Samples per second} = \frac{64{,}000}{8} = 8{,}000 \text{ samples/sec}$$

**Step 2: Find sampling interval**

$$\text{Interval} = \frac{1}{8{,}000} = 0.000125 \text{ seconds} = 125 \text{ μs}$$
