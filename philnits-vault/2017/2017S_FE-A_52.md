# 2017S_FE-A_52
According to PMBOK, which of the following combinations of processes pertains to project integration management? A. Control costs B. Develop project charter C. Direct and manage project work D. Identify risks E. Manage communications F. Perform integrated change control

a) A, B, E
b) B, C, E
c) B, C, F
d) C, D, F
?
c) B, C, F
### Explanation

**Project Integration Management** processes in PMBOK include:
- **Develop Project Charter** (B) ✅
- **Direct and Manage Project Work** (C) ✅
- **Perform Integrated Change Control** (F) ✅

- **Control Costs** (A) → Cost Management
- **Identify Risks** (D) → Risk Management
- **Manage Communications** (E) → Communications Management
