# 2017A_FE-A_3

Which of the following expressions in postfix notation is equivalent to an expression $2+5\times3+4$ in infix notation? Here, the operator '×' has higher precedence than '+'.

a) $253\ 4\times++$
b) $25+34\times+$
c) $253\times4++$
d) $253\times+4+$
?
d) $253\times+4+$
### Explanation

**Step 1: Apply operator precedence**

× has higher precedence than +, so the expression evaluates as:

$$2 + (5 \times 3) + 4$$

**Step 2: Convert to postfix (Reverse Polish Notation)**

- $5 \times 3$ → `5 3 ×`
- $2 + (5 \times 3)$ → `2 5 3 × +`
- $(2 + 5 \times 3) + 4$ → `2 5 3 × + 4 +`

This matches option D: $253\times+4+$
