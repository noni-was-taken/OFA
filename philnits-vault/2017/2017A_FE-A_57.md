# 2017A_FE-A_57

Which of the following device is used for protecting power supply circuits, communication devices, or other equipment from the overvoltage and the over current generated from the overvoltage?

a) AVR
b) CVCF
c) SPD
d) UPS
?
c) SPD
### Explanation

An **SPD (Surge Protection Device)** is specifically designed to protect electrical circuits from **voltage spikes and surges** — such as those caused by lightning strikes or power switching transients — absorbing and diverting excess voltage before it can damage equipment.

The other devices serve different purposes:
- **AVR** (Automatic Voltage Regulator) → maintains stable output voltage against gradual fluctuations, not sudden spikes
- **CVCF** (Constant Voltage Constant Frequency) → maintains stable voltage and frequency for sensitive equipment
- **UPS** (Uninterruptible Power Supply) → provides battery backup during power outages
