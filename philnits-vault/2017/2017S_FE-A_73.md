# 2017S_FE-A_73
Which of the following is an explanation of the long tail?

a) Generally known as the 80:20 empirical rule, the name derives from the phenomenon by which 80% of a company's sales are accounted for by the best-selling 20% of its products...
b) If 'value of network' is determined as the usefulness of the mutual access by the subscribers of the network,' then the value of the network is approximately proportional to the square of the number of users.
c) It enables a company to share the management resources through conducting multiple business activities simultaneously...
d) It is effective in reducing the risk of opportunity loss in an online shop that uses the Internet by lining up a broad range of items, including products for which sales opportunities are few.
?
d) It is effective in reducing the risk of opportunity loss in an online shop that uses the Internet by lining up a broad range of items, including products for which sales opportunities are few.
### Explanation

The **Long Tail** theory describes how online retailers can profitably sell a large variety of niche products in small quantities. Collectively, low-volume niche products can rival or exceed the sales of bestsellers.

- Option a) describes the **Pareto Principle (80/20 rule)**
- Option b) describes **Metcalfe's Law**
- Option c) describes **economies of scope**
