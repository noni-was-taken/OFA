# 2017S_FE-A_74
Which of the following performs processing according to steps (1) through (3)? (1) Based on the types and quantities as well as the parts list of the product planned to be manufactured in a fixed period of time in the future, the required quantity of components is calculated. (2) The net order quantity of each component is calculated from the amount of inventory that can be allocated. (3) The ordering time of the components is determined while considering the manufacturing and procurement lead time.

a) CAD (Computer Aided Design)
b) CRP (Capacity Requirements Planning)
c) JIT (Just In Time)
d) MRP (Material Requirements Planning)
?
d) MRP (Material Requirements Planning)
### Explanation

**MRP (Material Requirements Planning)** is a production planning system that:
1. Calculates **what materials are needed** and in what quantities (from the Bill of Materials)
2. Determines **net requirements** by subtracting existing inventory
3. Schedules **when to order** materials based on lead times

This precisely matches the three steps described.
