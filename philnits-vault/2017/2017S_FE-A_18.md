# 2017S_FE-A_18
Round robin (with a time allocation of 3 seconds) is used as a scheduling method for three processes in the order of P1, P2, and P3. What is the total waiting time (in seconds) of P3 that elapses before its execution completes?

| Process | Execution Time | Arrival Time |
|---------|---------------|--------------|
| P1      | 24 s          | 0 s          |
| P2      | 15 s          | 10 s         |
| P3      | 12 s          | 13 s         |

a) 35
b) 38
c) 45
d) 48
?
a) 35
### Explanation

Tracing Round Robin (quantum = 3s), P3 arrives at t=13 and its execution completes at t=48.

**Turnaround time** for P3 = $48 - 13 = 35$ seconds.

The question asks for "total waiting time that elapses before its execution completes," which is interpreted as the **turnaround time = 35 seconds** (total elapsed time from arrival to completion).
