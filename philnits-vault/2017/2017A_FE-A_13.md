# 2017A_FE-A_13

Which of the following is the RAID level that can recover data from the other disks, with a minimum of two-disk configuration?

a) RAID 0
b) RAID 1
c) RAID 5
d) RAID 6
?
b) RAID 1
### Explanation

| RAID Level | Redundancy | Minimum Disks |
|------------|-----------|---------------|
| RAID 0 | None (striping only) | 2 |
| **RAID 1** | **Full mirror — complete copy on 2nd disk** | **2** |
| RAID 5 | Distributed parity | 3 |
| RAID 6 | Dual parity | 4 |

**RAID 1** is the only level that provides fault tolerance (data recovery capability) with a minimum of just **2 disks** through mirroring.
