# 2017A_FE-A_58

Which of the following is an appropriate precaution to be observed when monitoring the use of the capacity and performance of an online system in the capacity management process of IT service management?

a) Incidents concerning capacity and performance are recorded.
b) Multiple measurement items such as the response time and CPU utilization are monitored on a regular basis.
c) Only the maximum value of performance data during a fixed period is focused upon, and deviation from the management limit is checked.
d) Since the measurement of performance during online hours causes a decline in service level, measurement is performed during offline hours.
?
b) Multiple measurement items such as the response time and CPU utilization are monitored on a regular basis.
### Explanation

Effective capacity management requires monitoring **multiple metrics** (response time, CPU utilization, memory, throughput) **on a regular basis** to identify trends, anticipate bottlenecks, and act before service levels are impacted.

The other options are incorrect:
- **A** → describes incident recording, not a monitoring practice
- **C** → focusing only on maximums misses averages and trends; a comprehensive view is needed
- **D** → performance must be measured **during live operation** to capture real conditions; offline measurement gives misleading results
