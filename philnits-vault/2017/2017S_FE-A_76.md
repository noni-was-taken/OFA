# 2017S_FE-A_76
Which of the following is a management organization that has a characteristic of both project and functional organizations to flexibly respond to changes in the business environment?

a) Divisional organization
b) Hierarchical organization
c) Line and staff organization
d) Matrix organization
?
d) Matrix organization
### Explanation

A **matrix organization** combines **functional** (permanent departments) and **project** (temporary teams) structures. Employees report to both a functional manager and a project manager, providing flexibility.

- **Divisional**: organized around products, regions, or customer segments
- **Hierarchical**: traditional top-down command structure
- **Line and staff**: line managers have authority; staff provide advisory roles
