# 2017S_FE-A_5
The expression shown below is written in postfix (reverse Polish) notation. What is the value of this expression?

`100 5 6 2 + * 12 4 / - - 36 -`

a) 18
b) 21
c) 27
d) 5
?
c) 27
### Explanation

Evaluate left to right using a stack:

1. Push `100`, `5`, `6`, `2`
2. `+`: pop 6, 2 → $6+2=8$; stack: `[100, 5, 8]`
3. `*`: pop 5, 8 → $5 \times 8=40$; stack: `[100, 40]`
4. Push `12`, `4`
5. `/`: pop 12, 4 → $12/4=3$; stack: `[100, 40, 3]`
6. `-`: pop 40, 3 → $40-3=37$; stack: `[100, 37]`
7. `-`: pop 100, 37 → $100-37=63$; stack: `[63]`
8. Push `36`
9. `-`: pop 63, 36 → $63-36=\mathbf{27}$

Result: **27**
