# 2017A_FE-A_54

Company A is going to launch a new software product and the sales team then has to demonstrate it to the customers. There is a risk that the sales team does not understand the product and cannot provide good demonstrations. As a consequence, the sales team will make fewer sales and there will be less revenue for the company. So, the management decided to provide good training for the sales team. Which of the following is the best method to manage the risk?

a) Risk acceptance
b) Risk exploitation
c) Risk mitigation
d) Risk transfer
?
c) Risk mitigation
### Explanation

The four negative risk response strategies are:

| Strategy | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Risk acceptance | Acknowledge and take no action |
| **Risk mitigation** | **Reduce the probability or impact** |
| Risk transfer | Shift the risk to a third party |
| Risk avoidance | Eliminate the risk entirely |

Providing training **reduces the probability** that the sales team will perform poorly. This is **risk mitigation**.

Risk exploitation applies to **positive risks (opportunities)**, not negative risks.
