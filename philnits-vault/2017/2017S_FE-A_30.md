# 2017S_FE-A_30
Among the phases of database design, which of the following phases involves the creation of an ER diagram?

a) Conceptual design of databases phase
b) Distributed design of databases phase
c) Logical design of databases phase
d) Physical design of databases phase
?
a) Conceptual design of databases phase
### Explanation

An **ER (Entity-Relationship) diagram** is created during the **conceptual design phase**. At this stage, designers identify key entities, attributes, and relationships without concern for the specific DBMS or implementation details.

- **Logical design**: converts the ER model into a relational schema
- **Physical design**: defines storage structures, indexes, and hardware-specific optimizations
- **Distributed design**: concerns how data is distributed across multiple sites
