# 2017S_FE-A_38
Which of the following is an appropriate term associated with the fraudulent action of impersonating an authorized person?

a) Destruction
b) Falsification
c) Spoofing
d) Tapping
?
c) Spoofing
### Explanation

**Spoofing** is the act of **impersonating** a legitimate user, device, or system to gain unauthorized access or deceive others.

- **Destruction**: deliberate damage or erasure of data/systems
- **Falsification**: altering or forging data or documents
- **Tapping**: intercepting communications (eavesdropping)
