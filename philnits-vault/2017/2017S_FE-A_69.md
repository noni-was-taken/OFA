# 2017S_FE-A_69
Which of the following is a marketing technique to create demands from consumers by using advertisements that improve the brand image of a company or a product?

a) Direct marketing
b) Relationship marketing
c) Pull strategy
d) Push strategy
?
c) Pull strategy
### Explanation

A **pull strategy** creates consumer demand through **advertising and brand promotion**, motivating customers to actively seek out the product.

- **Push strategy**: pushing products through the supply chain via promotions to distributors
- **Direct marketing**: communicating directly with individual consumers
- **Relationship marketing**: building long-term customer loyalty
