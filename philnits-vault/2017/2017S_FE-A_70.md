# 2017S_FE-A_70
PEST analysis is used for analyzing the external environment in determining a business strategy. What are the external environmental factors the PEST analysis analyzes?

a) Politics, Economics, Society, Technology
b) Politics, Enterprises, Society, Time
c) Price, Economics, Satisfaction, Technology
d) Price, Enterprises, Safety, Time
?
a) Politics, Economics, Society, Technology
### Explanation

**PEST analysis** examines **macro-environmental factors**:
- **P**olitical: government policies, regulations, stability
- **E**conomic: economic growth, inflation, exchange rates
- **S**ocial: cultural trends, demographics, lifestyle changes
- **T**echnological: innovation, R&D, automation

Sometimes extended to **PESTLE** (adding Legal and Environmental).
