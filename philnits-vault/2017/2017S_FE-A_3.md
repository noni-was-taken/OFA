# 2017S_FE-A_3
There are 16 cards numbered from 1 to 16. When four cards are randomly chosen at the same time, what is the probability that all of them are even?

a) $\frac{1}{26}$
b) $\frac{1}{24}$
c) $\frac{1}{20}$
d) $\frac{1}{16}$
?
a) $\frac{1}{26}$
### Explanation

- Even numbers from 1–16: {2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, 14, 16} → **8 even numbers**
- Ways to choose 4 from 8 even cards: $C(8,4) = 70$
- Ways to choose any 4 from 16 cards: $C(16,4) = 1820$
- Probability: $\frac{70}{1820} = \frac{1}{26}$
