# 2017A_FE-A_77

Which of the following items should be put under 'Liabilities and Stockholder's Equity' in a balance sheet?

a) Cash
b) Common stock
c) Fixed assets
d) Inventories
?
b) Common stock
### Explanation

A balance sheet has two sides:
- **Assets** (left side): what the company owns — cash, inventories, fixed assets
- **Liabilities and Stockholder's Equity** (right side): how those assets were financed

**Stockholder's Equity** includes common stock, retained earnings, and additional paid-in capital.

- Cash → Current asset
- **Common stock** → Stockholder's Equity ✓
- Fixed assets → Non-current asset
- Inventories → Current asset
