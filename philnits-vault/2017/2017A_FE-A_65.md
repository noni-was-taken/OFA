# 2017A_FE-A_65

The purchase of two types of IT devices A and B is being examined. The payout period is set in consideration of the useful life of each of the devices. Which of the following is an appropriate description for the minimum amount of annual profit necessary to recover the amount of investment within the set payout period for A and B? Here, the annual profit is made evenly every year, and the interest rate is not considered. [Table: Amount of investment (10,000 yen) A 90 B 300 | Payout period (years) A 3 B 5]

a) It is necessary for A and B to make the same amount of annual profit.
b) It is necessary for A to make twice the annual profit than that of B.
c) It is necessary for B to make 1.5 times the annual profit than that of A.
d) It is necessary for B to make twice the annual profit than that of A.
?
d) It is necessary for B to make twice the annual profit than that of A.
### Explanation

Calculate the minimum annual profit for each device:

$$\text{Annual profit (A)} = \frac{90}{3} = 30 \text{ 万円/year}$$

$$\text{Annual profit (B)} = \frac{300}{5} = 60 \text{ 万円/year}$$

$$\frac{B}{A} = \frac{60}{30} = 2$$

B requires **twice** the annual profit of A.
