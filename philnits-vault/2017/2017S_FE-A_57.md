# 2017S_FE-A_57
According to ITIL, which of the following is an activity or process in the Service Transition?

a) Assessing the IT recovery options of the new IT infrastructure considering the business risks and cost to the project
b) Classifying events once it is detected and communicating specific information to corresponding groups as an immediate action
c) Defining the reliability and service life of the IT infrastructure considering projected growth in the service
d) Implementing multiple strategies such as web seminars, newsletters, and visualization in transferring knowledge
?
d) Implementing multiple strategies such as web seminars, newsletters, and visualization in transferring knowledge
### Explanation

**ITIL Service Transition** covers building, testing, and deploying services into production. **Knowledge Management** is a core process — ensuring the right information is available at the right time through training and communication strategies.

- Option a) relates to **IT Service Continuity Management** (Service Design)
- Option b) describes **Event Management** (Service Operation)
- Option c) relates to **Availability/Capacity Management** (Service Design)
