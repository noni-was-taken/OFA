# 2017S_FE-A_59
Which of the following is an appropriate activity that is undertaken by a system auditor who audits access control?

a) Confirmation of the management status of access control concerning data
b) Creation and storage of a management table for access control concerning software
c) Establishment of a management policy for access control concerning the network
d) Implementation of operations for access control concerning hardware
?
a) Confirmation of the management status of access control concerning data
### Explanation

A **system auditor's** role is to **independently verify and evaluate** whether controls are in place and working. **Confirming the management status** of access controls is an audit activity (observation, review, verification).

- Options b), c), and d) are **management or operational activities** — an auditor should remain independent and not implement policies or operations.
