# 2017S_FE-A_72
Which of the following is an explanation of benchmarking used for corporate management?

a) It refers to a qualitative and quantitative understanding of a company's own products, services, and operation through comparison with those of competitors or advanced companies.
b) It refers to drastically reforming the quality and structure of a company by redesigning its business processes from a customer viewpoint and by taking full advantage of information technology.
c) It refers to the ability to plan and manage the allocation of company-wide management resources in an effective and integrated manner and to achieve an improvement in management efficiency.
d) It refers to the concentration of management resources on the unique skills and technologies of a company that can generate profit and that are superior to those of other companies.
?
a) It refers to a qualitative and quantitative understanding of a company's own products, services, and operation through comparison with those of competitors or advanced companies.
### Explanation

**Benchmarking** measures a company's own performance against **industry best practices or competitors** to identify gaps and opportunities.

- Option b) describes **BPR (Business Process Reengineering)**
- Option c) describes **ERP** or integrated resource management
- Option d) describes **core competency** strategy
