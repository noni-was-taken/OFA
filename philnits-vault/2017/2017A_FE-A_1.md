# 2017A_FE-A_1

What is the octal representation of a hexadecimal fraction F1B0.C?

a) 170660.6
b) 361540.14
c) 743300.6
d) 5213052.3
?
a) 170660.6
### Explanation

**Step 1: Convert each hex digit to 4-bit binary**

| Hex | Binary |
|-----|--------|
| F | 1111 |
| 1 | 0001 |
| B | 1011 |
| 0 | 0000 |
| . | . |
| C | 1100 |

Full binary string: `1111000110110000.1100`

**Step 2: Group into sets of 3 bits for octal, from the decimal point outward**

Integer part (pad left with zeros to complete groups of 3):
`001 111 000 110 110 000` → **1 7 0 6 6 0** → `170660`

Fractional part (pad right to complete groups of 3):
`.110 0` → `.110 000` → **6 0** → but the significant part is `.6`

**Result: 170660.6**
