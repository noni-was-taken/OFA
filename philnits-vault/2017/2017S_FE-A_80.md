# 2017S_FE-A_80
Which of the following is not protected by 'copyright' that is based on the World Trade Organization's Agreement on Trade-Related Aspects of Intellectual Property Rights (TRIPS)?

a) Database
b) Free software publicly available on the Internet
c) Programming language and its conventions
d) Software operation manual
?
c) Programming language and its conventions
### Explanation

Under copyright law (including TRIPS), **ideas, concepts, methods, and systems are not protected** — only specific expressions. A **programming language and its conventions** (syntax, grammar, rules) are abstract systems/methods, not copyrightable expression.

- **Databases**: protected as compilations
- **Free/open source software**: copyright still applies; the author grants liberal use rights
- **Software operation manuals**: protected as literary works
