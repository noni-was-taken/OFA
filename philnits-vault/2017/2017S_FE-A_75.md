# 2017S_FE-A_75
Which of the following is the procurement process that enables suppliers to bid against one another on the Internet to sell their products or services under the conditions specified by a general customer?

a) B to B
b) G to C
c) Reverse auction
d) Virtual mall
?
c) Reverse auction
### Explanation

A **reverse auction** has **one buyer and many sellers bidding *down*** on price to win a contract. The buyer specifies requirements; suppliers compete by lowering their prices.

- **B to B**: general business-to-business commerce
- **G to C**: government-to-citizen e-services
- **Virtual mall**: an online marketplace hosting multiple sellers
