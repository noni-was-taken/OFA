# 2017S_FE-A_56
According to the ISO/IEC 20000-1, which of the following is a service delivery process that the service provider shall agree the services to be delivered and shall agree a catalogue of services with the customer?

a) Capacity management
b) Information security management
c) Service continuity and availability management
d) Service level management
?
d) Service level management
### Explanation

**Service Level Management (SLM)** is responsible for negotiating, agreeing, documenting, and monitoring IT service levels. This includes defining a **service catalogue** and establishing **SLAs**.

- **Capacity management**: ensures IT infrastructure meets current and future demand
- **Information security management**: protects confidentiality, integrity, and availability
- **Service continuity and availability management**: ensures services can be maintained or recovered after disruption
