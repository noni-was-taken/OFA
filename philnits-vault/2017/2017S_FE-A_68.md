# 2017S_FE-A_68
Companies are classified based on their competitive position in the market. There are typically four types: leader, challenger, follower, and nicher. Which of the following is an appropriate characteristic of the strategy of a leader?

a) A full frontal matching attack against the competitor's product, advertising, price and distribution, or indirect attack on the competitor's weaknesses or on gaps in the competitor's market coverage
b) Capturing a specific market that is not targeted by a high-ranking company and concentrating management resources for securing and maintaining the predominance in the market
c) Determining the real ability of the challenger company in the market, and giving priority for securing stable profits rather than expanding the market share by doing as the leader company does
d) Proposing a new product and its usage method to the consumer to expand the total demand while maintaining or expanding the market share
?
d) Proposing a new product and its usage method to the consumer to expand the total demand while maintaining or expanding the market share
### Explanation

The **market leader** strategy focuses on **expanding the total market** and defending/growing its dominant share through innovation and new products.

- Option a) describes the **challenger** strategy
- Option b) describes the **nicher** strategy
- Option c) describes the **follower** strategy
