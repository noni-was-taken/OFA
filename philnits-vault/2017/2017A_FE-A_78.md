# 2017A_FE-A_78

Which of the following type of financial statements shows the financial position of an enterprise at a particular point of time?

a) Balance sheet
b) Cash flow statement
c) Income statement
d) Statements of shareholders' equity
?
a) Balance sheet
### Explanation

The **balance sheet** is a **snapshot** of a company's financial position at a **specific date** — showing what the company owns (assets), what it owes (liabilities), and shareholders' equity at that moment.

The other statements cover **periods of time**:
- **Cash flow statement** → cash inflows and outflows over a period
- **Income statement** → revenues and expenses over a period
- **Statements of shareholders' equity** → changes in equity over a period
