# 2025A_FE-A_13
Five processes A, B, C, D and E arrive at almost the same time in the order of A, B, C, D and E. The processes have estimated running times of 10, 6, 2, 8, and 4 milliseconds, respectively. Which of the following is the average process turnaround time with the First-Come-First-Served (FCFS) scheduling algorithm?

a) 14
b) 19.2
c) 20
d) 22
?
c) 20
### Explanation

Under **FCFS**, processes run in arrival order: A → B → C → D → E

| Process | Burst | Finish Time | Turnaround Time |
|---------|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| A       | 10    | 10          | 10              |
| B       | 6     | 16          | 16              |
| C       | 2     | 18          | 18              |
| D       | 8     | 26          | 26              |
| E       | 4     | 30          | 30              |

Average turnaround = $\frac{10 + 16 + 18 + 26 + 30}{5} = \frac{100}{5} = \mathbf{20}$

### Answer: c) 20
